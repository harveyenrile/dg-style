A Tough Act to Follow in Japanese Buffets

Certainly worth the price of admission, so to speak. The sashimi was fresh, not frozen. The tempura was cooked properly and didn't make you feel you were just eating pieces of cold batter masquerading as tempura. The teppanyaki station was excellent, their steak and salmon were especially good. It was my first time to have wasabi ice cream and it did not disappoint. The tart spiciness of the wasabi blended well with the sweet taste of the cream. Definitely will be a repeat diner.
