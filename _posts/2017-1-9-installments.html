Frequently Asked Questions

How does installment work?

Installments are available for purchases above P10,000. You'll be charged the first month during your purchase, then second month then the third month.

Credit card error during installment

If your credit card failed to push through with the monthly installment, you will be asked to update your credit card details. Failure to do so will result in disabled coupons.
